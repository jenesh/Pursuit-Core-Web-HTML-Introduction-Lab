The Perfect PB&J

Description

This peanut butter sandwich is literally to die for. Like if you die without eating this in your one and only life, you didn't really live.

Ingredients

  • Two fine looking bread(little mold is fine)
  • One big ol' carton of seedless grape jelly
  • One creamy yet crunchy jar of peanut butter
  • One asthetic round ceramic plate that is not made of plastic
  • One sharp knife that can cut through steel as well as bread
  • One hungry "I don't think I'm allergic to peanuts" looking person

Directions

  1. Get two perfectly sliced breads and lay them down like they're under arrest
  2. Grab the knife by the sharpest yet smoothest edge
  3. Open both the seedless grape jelly and creamy yey crunchy peanut butter
  4. Using the knife get some peanut butter and spread it on the left bread
  5. Using the knife get some graphe jelly and spread it on the right bread
  6. Close the bread up using your hands and split it diagonally using the knife
  7. Squeeze the bread so a lil bit of jelly plops out
  8. Finish one of the sandwich in three bites and the other in four
